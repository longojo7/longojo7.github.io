My fifth week continued the initial coding phase and culminated in our first team collaboration session to compare codes
and identify emerging themes. Here's a summary of what I accomplished:

- **Initial Coding:** CCompleted the first round of independent coding on the 10 sampled files in MaxQDA, producing 184

questions. It was definitely a difficult process doing this for the first time, and looking back, I can see some labels

first 10 files. I walked through my most prominent and frequently labeled codes, as well as the themes I picked up on
during this initial phase. Sakib and Madiha also shared their code systems, and we exchanged feedback and critiques on
each other’s work. My initial pass was strong, and my next step is to go back through and group my codes into broader
categories before we meet in person to establish a more mature codebook.

While this has been one of the more challenging weeks learning a new skill, it has also been one of the most informative
as we start to really dig into the meaning of our data. I'm very excited for the next couple of weeks, and stay tuned for
updates!
